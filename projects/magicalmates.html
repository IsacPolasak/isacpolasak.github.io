Magical Mates

A magical companion for stories, routines, and connections.

Project Overview

Product: Magical Mates is a smart stuffed animal and app that helps children build routines and spark imagination. Parents use the app to send voice messages and stories, which are delivered through the plush toy using AI-generated audio.

Problem: Parents want to reduce screen time while staying connected and supporting routines. Most tools lack emotional warmth or imaginative play.

Goal: Create a magical, screen-free experience that helps families stay connected, build routines, and spark imagination through smart, voice-enabled plush toys.
