Hi, It's Isac!

I’m a UX/UI designer with a love for language, and a good knitting project (or two... or three... shhh don't judge) always in the works. I split my time between Seattle, NYC, and Florida, all of which are home in different ways.

I got into UX because I’m fascinated by how people think, what they need, and why something works (or doesn’t). I’m naturally analytical, but I also love being creative, which is why design just makes sense for me. I get hooked on the research, the exploration, and the “aha” moments when an idea finally clicks. I’m especially drawn to hands-on, tactile creativity (hence the knitting), and I’m the kind of person who actually gets excited about finding a better, smarter way to do something.

I believe good design isn’t just about making things look nice. It’s about making things make sense, feel good, and actually work. I’m not afraid to try, test, break, and rebuild until something feels right. I’m always learning, evolving, and asking questions. That’s what keeps me going, and what I hope to bring into every project I work on.
